Peanut Butter and Banana Smoothie

Photo by Elena Leya on Unsplash

Description

Delicious high-protein peanut butter and banana smoothie for breakfast or pre-workout.

Ingredients

  • 2 bananas, broken into chunks
  • 2 cups milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons honey
  • 2 cups ice cubes
  • 1 scoop of protein powder

Steps

  1. Place everything in the blender.
  2. Blend until smooth, about 30 seconds.
  3. Enjoy!
