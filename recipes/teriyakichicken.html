Teriyaki Chicken

Photo by Tomoyo S on Unsplash

Description

Delicious juicy japanese inspired home-style sirfry chicken recipe.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts or Thighs
  • Soy Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Honey
  • White Wine Vinegar
  • Cornflour
  • Garlic Powder

Steps

  1. Mix cornflour with soy sauce, honey, and vinegar.
  2. Heat oil in a frying pan.
  3. Add chicken chunks and cook for about 10-15 minutes until the chicken is almost cooked through.
  4. Add garlic powder.
  5. Add sauce mixture and cook for about 5 minutes until everything is well coated and thickened.
